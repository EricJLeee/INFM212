Return and Exchange Policy

You may return merchandise or packaged tea within 30 days of your purchase date. Refunds will be made in the form of your original payment, unless you have used PayPal to make your original purchase. For PayPal purchases, your refund will be in the form of Xiang Herbs & Tea Credit.

For products that have been opened or damaged, they may still be returned or exchanged, but may incur a restocking fee up to 20%.
